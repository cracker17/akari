Frequently asked questions about Akari lighting

A pendant or cluster pendant hung 28–34 inches above the table surface creates the ideal dining atmosphere. Warm white (2700–3000K) colour temperature brings out rich tones in food and creates an intimate, inviting ambiance. Akari's Jewels & Pearls and Sabi Cluster Pendant are both ideal for dining rooms of any size.
A simple rule: add the length and width of your room in feet, and the result in inches is your ideal fixture diameter. For example, a 12 × 14 ft room suits a 26-inch pendant. Over a dining table, your fixture should be no wider than the table minus 12 inches on each side.
For dining tables, hang the bottom of the fixture 28–36 inches above the table. In open spaces or hallways, the bottom of the pendant should be at least 7 feet from the floor. All Akari pendants have fully adjustable cord lengths (up to 120 cm) to suit any ceiling height.
